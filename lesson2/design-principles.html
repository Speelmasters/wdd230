Hick's Law

Instagram

Instagram.com

Hick's law is states that the more option available, has a exponential increase in the amount of time it takes to choose an option. I feel instagram is a great choice since the options are relatively limited on the homepage, and categorizes and serches into generalizations so it it is easier to choose.

Fitt's Law

Softonic

Softonic.com

While the desktop page isn't as great, it is clearly shown in the image that the button has a vibrant button that is centered on the page and extends to both sides. Considering that Fitt' law is about the decrease in users clicking a button based on its size and location, this would definately not detract from clicks.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

Apple.com

Apple is the paragon of white space and clean design in my opinion. Their pages aren't cluttered and the leave open area around each item to not draw away from the product.
