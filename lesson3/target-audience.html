Target Audience

THe target adience of this website will be business onwers and euterepenuers who want to connect with businesses in the area, as well as displaying the nearby attractions and tourist locations.

Personas

Persona 1

Neil Adams is a 41 pear old father of 2, and loving husband to his wife, Clara. He owns 2 strip malls in the Coeur d'Alene area, where he leases out the areas to stores. He loves to spend time with his family when he isn't working part time at an auto shop. he stays up to date on laws and regulations for the area to keep his buildings up to code.

Persona 2

Irene Green is a 30 year old scientific reasercher, who enjoys listening to music, playing card games and cookery. She is intelligent and reliable, but can also be very rude and a bit untidy. She is visiting the area with her boyfriend of 2 years, and hopes he will propose on the trip. She hopes to find some places to have fun and take great phots when the time is right.

Scenarios

  1. A local business owner is looking to join the Chamber of Commerce and wants to know how that is handled.

  2. A tourist company wants to know what places there are to visit while in the area so they can plan trips around those attractions or stops.

  3. A local business owner has opened a tourist attraction and is looking to get it on the website. They want to know what constitutes an attraction, and if there are any costs.

  4. A local community group wants to help with a charity in the area. They need the charities' contact information, as well as location and help availablility.

  5. An apartment developmnet company is build in a new area, and is looking to find business owners who would like to open shop in the area to promote growth.
